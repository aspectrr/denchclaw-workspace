**Last Updated:** 2026-03-31
**Source:** Bloomberry (767 Elastic customers analyzed) + Elastic.co customer stories

## Executive Summary

Companies using Elastic/ELK share one thing: **they manage complexity at scale**. They're handling millions of events, need 99.999% uptime, and can't afford observability blind spots.

**Best ICP Fit:** Seed-stage to mid-size companies (51-200 employees) in software development, financial services, or security — with Director/VP-level buyers and DevOps/SRE practitioners.
