#ICP: Full-time platform engineers, small teams, mission-critical ELK stacks, non-contract

## How to Use This Tracker

**Goal:** Learn from peers, don't sell. Ask about their pain, not your product.

**Message Template:**
> "Hi [Name], I'm an ELK engineer too - currently working on [relevant topic]. Curious - what's the thing you spend the most time on with your ELK stack? On-call? Pipeline debugging? Something else? Just trying to understand what's top of mind for other engineers."

**Key Questions to Ask:**
1. What's your biggest time sink with ELK?
2. What wakes you up at 3am? (on-call pain)
3. How do you currently debug pipeline issues?
4. What would save you the most time?
5. What's the most frustrating part of your job?

## What NOT to Ask

- Don't mention building something
- Don't pitch a product
- Don't ask about budget
- Don't ask for referrals
- Don't schedule follow-ups (unless they offer)

## What TO Ask

### Core Pain Questions
- What's your biggest time sink with ELK?
- What part of your day is most frustrating?
- What's the hardest part of on-call?
- If you could automate one ELK task, what would it be?
- What do you wish you never had to do manually?

### Deep Dive Questions
- Walk me through how you'd debug a slow query
- What Logstash pipeline issues do you see most often?
- How do you handle Kafka lag affecting your ELK pipeline?
- What's your process for investigating a DLQ?
- How do you diagnose stuck shards?

### Leverage & Growth Questions
- How much of your week is maintenance vs building new things?
- If you had 2x the team, what would you tackle?
- What's the request you get most from other teams?
- What would free up the most time for you to work on higher-value work?

### Tooling & Workflow
- What does your current alerting setup look like?
- How do you track ELK health — dashboards, automation, manual?
- What would make debugging faster for you?
- Do you use Ansible/Terraform for ELK infrastructure?

### Stack-Specific Questions
- Self-managed or Elastic Cloud? (Critical for ICP)
- What version are you on?
- Do you use Fleet/Elastic Agent or legacy Beats?
- Kafka in your pipeline? Logstash or Elasticsearch Ingest?

### On-Call Questions
- Are you on-call for ELK?
- How often are you paged for ELK issues?
- What's the worst ELK incident you've had?
- Does ELK on-call affect your sleep/work-life balance?
