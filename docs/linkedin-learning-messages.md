## Notes

### Connection Quality Tracking
After each conversation, update the **Connection Quality** field:
- **Great Fit** — Mission-critical ELK stack, full-time, small team
- **Good Fit** — Solid ICP but one minor gap (company size TBD)
- **OK Fit** — Conversation was useful but not ICP
- **Bad Fit** — Not our target person
- **Not ICP** — Politely move on

### Follow-Up Strategy
1. Wait for connection acceptance
2. Send message within 24 hours
3. Keep it short — 5 sentences max
4. Focus on curiosity, not selling
5. Ask about pain, not solutions
6. Track conversations in CRM

### What NOT to Do
- Don't pitch the product
- Don't ask for a meeting
- Don't send links to your website
- Don't follow up more than once if they don't respond
